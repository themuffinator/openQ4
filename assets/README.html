About

OpenQ4 is a free, open-source replacement for the Quake 4 engine that brings your classic game into the modern era. Built on the shoulders of Quake4Doom, it keeps everything you love about the original — the brutal combat, the tight gameplay, the iconic atmosphere — and layers on a fresh set of visuals and quality-of-life upgrades that make it feel right at home on today's hardware.

Plug in a controller and play from the couch, enjoy crisp widescreen and ultrawide support, or push the visuals further with HDR rendering, dynamic shadow maps, and a suite of post-processing effects. The best part? It all runs on your existing copy of Quake 4 — no new assets, no subscription, just your game looking and playing better than ever.

OpenQ4 does not include game assets. You must own a legitimate copy of Quake 4 to play. The engine automatically detects your installation from Steam or GOG. OpenQ4 is not compatible with legacy Quake 4 game mods.

Installation

What You Need

  • A legitimate copy of Quake 4 — Steam or GOG
  • The latest OpenQ4 release from the Releases page
  • A modern 64-bit operating system (Windows, Linux, or macOS)
  1. Install Quake 4

    Purchase and install Quake 4 via Steam or GOG if you haven't already.

  2. Download OpenQ4

    Grab the latest release archive for your platform from the Releases page.

  3. Extract the archive

    Unzip the archive to a folder of your choice.

  4. Run OpenQ4

    Launch OpenQ4-client_x64.exe (Windows) or OpenQ4-client_x64 (Linux/macOS). The engine will auto-detect your Quake 4 installation.

Windows: The package is self-contained — no separate Visual C++ Redistributable or OpenAL install required.
Linux: The runtime currently uses X11/GLX. On Wayland, run OpenQ4 through XWayland (DISPLAY must be set).

Manual Path (if not auto-detected)

OpenQ4-client_x64 +set fs_basepath "/path/to/Quake 4"

Features

Modern rendering upgrades running with original Quake 4 assets.

Visual Enhancements

🎨

HDR Pipeline

FP16 scene and post-process targets with filmic tone mapping and full color controls — exposure, contrast, saturation, vibrance.

✨

Bloom

Tunable separable half-resolution bloom for stronger highlights with lower full-screen cost.

🌑

Shadow Mapping (experimental)

Projected and point-light shadow maps, cascaded shadow maps (CSM), alpha-tested transparency shadows, and optional translucent shadow accumulation.

🌫️

SSAO

Screen-space ambient occlusion for added depth and contact shadowing in the final frame.

📺

CRT Emulation

Optional post-process with scanlines, phosphor mask, screen curvature, and chromatic aberration controls.

🔲

Modern AA & Upscaling

MSAA and SMAA 1x (medium preset), plus high-quality resolution-scaling paths and supersampling presets.

Display and Controls

🎮

Controller Support

Full gamepad/joystick support with hotplug, dual-stick analog movement and look, and complete button mapping.

🖥️

Multi-Monitor Support

Target specific displays, auto-detect the active monitor, and keep UI behavior sane across modern desktop setups.

📐

Auto Aspect Ratio

UI, FOV, zoom, and view-weapon framing adapt from live render size — no manual aspect-ratio toggles needed.

🪟

Modern Display Modes

Fullscreen, borderless windowed, exclusive fullscreen, and desktop-native fullscreen paths.

Engine and Platform

🎯

Full Game Support

Complete single-player campaign and multiplayer modes in one unified engine stack under a single openq4/ directory.

📦

Stock Asset Compatibility

Runs against official Quake 4 PK4s — no replacement content shipped. Auto-validates PK4 checksums at startup.

🔊

Audio

WAV and Ogg Vorbis support via OpenAL Soft for high-quality 3D audio.

🛡️

Crash Diagnostics

Automatic crash dumps and structured logs for easier debugging and issue reporting.

Compatibility Status

Reflects compatibility with official Quake 4 assets (q4base PK4s).

  • ✅BSE Reconstruction — Core BSE runtime rebuilt and integrated so stock effects execute through the OpenQ4 pipeline
  • ✅Sound Shaders — Effect-driven sound shader paths restored
  • ✅Screen Effects — BSE-driven screen/camera effects operational
  • ✅Material Shaders — Material compatibility restored, removing reliance on custom overrides
  • ✅Modern Display Handling — Aspect-ratio, multi-monitor, and fullscreen paths integrated
  • ✅Script Stability — x64 pointer-temp write guards prevent interpreter corruption in trigger/door event chains
  • ❌Ongoing Sweep — Map-by-map gameplay validation continues to catch residual regressions
